About Me

I’m a Data Analyst with an MSc in Data Science from the University of Surrey, experienced in working with real-world data across both enterprise systems and operational environments.

At Accenture, I worked in a data and analytics role supporting Shell’s Downstream Trading operations. I analysed large transactional datasets using SQL and Python, built ETL workflows with Azure Data Factory, and developed Power BI dashboards to track KPIs, operational performance, and risk indicators for senior stakeholders. I also automated recurring reports and data validation checks, reducing manual effort and improving data reliability.

Alongside this, I currently support retail operations at M&S Simply Food (via SSP UK & Ireland), where I work with SKU-level sales, pricing, and inventory data. I reconcile system and Power BI reports with in-store counts, identify discrepancies, analyse trends in sales and waste, and prepare concise summaries to support day-to-day operational decision-making.
