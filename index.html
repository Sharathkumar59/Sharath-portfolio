About Me

I’m a Data Analyst with an MSc in Data Science from the University of Surrey and experience working with real-world data in both enterprise and operational environments.

At Accenture, I supported reporting and analytics for downstream trading operations, working with SQL, Python, Azure Data Factory, and Power BI to validate data quality, automate reporting, and build dashboards for stakeholders.

Alongside this, I currently support retail operations at M&S Simply Food (via SSP UK & Ireland), working with SKU-level sales, pricing, and inventory data. I reconcile system and Power BI reports with in-store counts to identify discrepancies, track trends in sales and waste, and support day-to-day decision-making using Excel and Power BI outputs.

Transferable skills: I’m comfortable working with ambiguous requests, prioritising tasks under time pressure, and communicating clearly with non-technical teams. I pay close attention to detail, challenge data that doesn’t look right, and focus on outcomes — not just analysis for its own sake.
